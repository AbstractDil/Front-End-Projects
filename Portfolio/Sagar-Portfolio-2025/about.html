About Me

Hello, I'm Sagar Nandy, a passionate and detail-oriented full stack developer with expertise in building dynamic and responsive web applications. My journey in technology began with a curiosity for how things work, and it has evolved into a career dedicated to creating efficient, scalable, and visually appealing digital experiences.

I have a strong foundation in PHP frameworks like CodeIgniter and Laravel, and I am also well-versed in modern JavaScript frameworks such as Vue.js. My skills extend to both relational databases like MySQL and NoSQL databases like MongoDB, allowing me to design and manage a wide range of data architectures.

I am committed to clean code, continuous learning, and collaborative problem-solving. I am always excited to take on new challenges and contribute to innovative projects that make a real impact.
